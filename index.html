The Person You Became to Survive

Understanding Your Past to Reclaim Your Present

There’s a version of you that was created for survival — the people-pleaser who learned to earn love, the overthinker who predicted danger, the perfectionist who tied worth to achievement. These adaptations kept you safe once, but now they feel like cages.

In this book I offer a compassionate, honest conversation — a companion for your late-night unravelling. Through storytelling and psychological insight you’ll learn to decode your patterns, transform anxiety, set boundaries, and reconnect with the person you were before survival shaped you.

If you’re ready to thank your survival self for its service and begin the journey home to you — this is the first step.
